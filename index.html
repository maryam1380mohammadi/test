🍖Kabab Koobideh🍖

Kobideh kebab is a type of kebab whose main ingredients are minced meat. This dish is often eaten with chelow.
This dish is one of the most popular Iranian dishes and usually has its own special cooking techniques.

Types and Serving Methods:
Kebab can be made with various meats such as: lamb, beef, chicken, and veal.
